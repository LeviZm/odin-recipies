Tacos de Carne Asada

These are steak tacos served on corn flour tortillas. They are a truck food delicacy. They come topped with cheese, cilantro, salsa, avocado, and a splash of squeezed lime.

Ingredients

  • 16 Oz. Steak
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Shredded or Grated Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sliced Avocados
  • Sliced Limes
